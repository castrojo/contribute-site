## New Information Architecture

Based on the meeting notes from "ALL THE DOCS~", the new structure emphasizes:

### 1. **Maintainers** (The Individual)
- CNCF project maintainers (sandbox → incubating → graduated)
- Individual contributor guidance
- Personal development paths

### 2. **Projects** (The Group) 
- Project-level guidance and resources
- Project governance and health
- Project lifecycle management

### 3. **Community** (The Group of Groups)
- CNCF Staff and new staff onboarding
- TAG leads and participants
- Cross-project initiatives

### 4. **Resources & Services**
- Mentorship programs
- Tools and services
- Documentation and templates

## Migration Principles

- **Correctness over longevity**: Ensure accuracy and relevance
- **Strong cross-referencing**: Create interconnected content
- **Canonical source approach**: Single source of truth for each topic
- **Service design mindset**: "We provide projects a service"
