# TAG Coordination

Guidelines for CNCF staff on how to effectively coordinate with and support Technical Advisory Groups (TAGs).

## Understanding TAGs

Technical Advisory Groups are self-organizing groups that focus on specific technical domains within cloud native computing. They:
- **Develop best practices** and guidance in their technical areas
- **Coordinate cross-project efforts** in their domains
- **Advise the TOC** on technical matters
- **Foster collaboration** between related projects

## Your Role in TAG Support

As CNCF staff, you support TAGs by:
- **Facilitating communication** between TAGs and the broader CNCF
- **Providing administrative support** for TAG operations
- **Connecting TAGs** with relevant projects and resources
- **Enabling TAG deliverables** through infrastructure and services

## Working with TAG Leadership

### TAG Chairs
- **Respect their autonomy** in technical decision-making
- **Support their coordination needs** with other groups
- **Facilitate their access** to CNCF resources and services
- **Assist with administrative tasks** that don't require technical expertise

### TAG Participants
- **Enable participation** through accessible meetings and documentation
- **Support contribution pathways** for new participants
- **Facilitate connections** between participants and relevant projects
- **Provide platforms** for sharing TAG work with the broader community

## Services You Can Provide

### Administrative Support
- **Meeting infrastructure** setup and management
- **Documentation hosting** and organization
- **Event coordination** for TAG-specific meetings
- **Communication channels** maintenance

### Technical Infrastructure
- **Repository management** for TAG deliverables
- **CI/CD setup** for automated processes
- **Website hosting** for TAG-specific content
- **Tool access** for collaboration and productivity

### Community Coordination
- **Cross-TAG communication** facilitation
- **Project outreach** for TAG initiatives
- **Conference coordination** for TAG presentations
- **Marketing support** for TAG deliverables

## Cross-TAG Coordination

### Identifying Overlap
- **Monitor TAG activities** for potential collaboration opportunities
- **Facilitate introductions** between TAGs with related work
- **Highlight dependencies** that might require coordination
- **Support joint initiatives** when requested by TAGs

### Avoiding Conflicts
- **Communicate proactively** about potential overlaps
- **Facilitate discussion** before conflicts arise
- **Provide neutral facilitation** if conflicts do occur
- **Escalate to TOC** when resolution requires governance decision

## Communication Patterns

### Regular Touchpoints
- **Monthly check-ins** with TAG chairs
- **Quarterly planning** coordination
- **Annual strategic** alignment discussions
- **Ad-hoc support** as needed

### Information Sharing
- **TAG updates** to CNCF staff team
- **Cross-TAG coordination** summaries
- **Resource availability** notifications
- **Community feedback** about TAG work

## Common Challenges

### Resource Constraints
- **Competing priorities** between TAG needs
- **Limited infrastructure** or budget
- **Volunteer time** management
- **Technical complexity** requiring specialized skills

### Coordination Issues
- **Overlapping scope** between TAGs
- **Different working styles** and preferences
- **Communication gaps** between groups
- **Timeline misalignment** for joint efforts

## Success Indicators

### TAG Health
- **Active participation** in TAG activities
- **Regular deliverables** and progress
- **Growing engagement** with the broader community
- **Effective collaboration** with related projects

### Your Support Effectiveness
- **Responsive service** delivery
- **Proactive problem** identification and resolution
- **Strong relationships** with TAG leadership
- **Successful coordination** of cross-TAG initiatives
