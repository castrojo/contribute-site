# CNCF Staff Onboarding

Welcome to the CNCF team! This guide will help you get started in your role supporting the cloud native community.

## Getting Started

### Your First Week
- Complete CNCF orientation and security training
- Set up access to internal systems and tools
- Meet your team and key stakeholders
- Review your role-specific responsibilities

### Understanding CNCF
- **Mission**: Advance cloud native computing
- **Projects**: Support 150+ hosted projects
- **Community**: Serve global developer community
- **Ecosystem**: Enable vendor-neutral collaboration

## Key Resources

### Internal Systems
- **Slack**: CNCF staff channels for coordination
- **Project Database**: Track project status and needs
- **Support Systems**: Help desk and escalation procedures
- **Documentation**: Internal processes and procedures

### Community Interaction
- **Project Liaison**: How to work with project maintainers
- **TAG Coordination**: Supporting Technical Advisory Groups
- **Event Support**: Conference and meetup assistance
- **External Communications**: Representing CNCF professionally

## Your Role in Supporting Projects

As CNCF staff, you provide critical services to our hosted projects:
- **Administrative Support**: Legal, trademark, and operational assistance
- **Technical Services**: Infrastructure, security scanning, and tools
- **Community Growth**: Marketing, events, and ecosystem development
- **Governance Support**: Process facilitation and conflict resolution
